### 1. AI Buyer Prompt Test

- “Invisalign dentist near [city]”

We identify whether AI tools mention your clinic or competitors for patient-intent searches.

### 3. Website AI Readiness Check

We check whether your website clearly communicates:

- who your clinic is
- where you operate
- what treatments you offer
- who your dentists/orthodontists are
- why patients should trust you
- whether your content is easy for AI tools to parse

### 4. Schema & Structured Data Review

We check for dental/local business schema including:

- Dentist / LocalBusiness schema
- address and phone data
- service/treatment markup
- FAQ schema
- review/rating signals
- practitioner information
- sameAs profile links

### 5. AI Crawler & Indexability Check

We check whether your site can be accessed by search and AI crawlers such as:

- Googlebot
- Bingbot
- GPTBot / OAI-SearchBot
- PerplexityBot
- ClaudeBot
- Applebot

### 6. Local Trust Signal Review

We look at signals that help AI tools trust your clinic:

- Google Business Profile strength
- reviews and rating signals
- dental directories
- dentist credentials
- AHPRA/GDC/local registration info where relevant
- testimonials
- before/after proof
- location consistency

### 7. 30-Day Fix Plan

You receive a prioritized list of fixes to improve your chances of being found, understood and recommended by AI search systems.
